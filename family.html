4.All the countries/places we have been traveled.

1. Visiting Singapore

Singapore is a modern and exciting city with lots to see and do.
The streets are very clean and safe, and it is easy to get around by public transport.
There are beautiful gardens, impressive skyscrapers, and colorful neighborhoods to explore.
The food is amazing, with many local dishes from Chinese, Malay, and Indian cultures.
Famous attractions include Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, Sentosa Island, and the Singapore Zoo.
Visiting Singapore is fun and full of new experiences for everyone.

2. Visiting Thailand

Thailand is a beautiful country in Southeast Asia, famous for its beaches, mountains, and rich culture.

The cities are lively, with markets, temples, and street food everywhere.

Visitors can enjoy Thai food, traditional festivals, and friendly people.

Famous places to visit include Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket, and the Phi Phi Islands.

Thailand is full of exciting experiences and is great for both adventure and relaxation.

3.Visiting China

As a visitor, China feels big, busy, and exciting, with modern cities like Shanghai, famous places like the Great Wall of China, crowded streets, bright lights, rich history, and delicious food everywhere.

4.Nha Trang (City in Vietnam)

As a visitor, Nha Trang feels relaxing and lively, with long sandy beaches, clear blue water, and gentle waves. You can enjoy fresh seafood, visit colorful markets, see the ancient Po Nagar Cham Towers, and explore islands nearby. The city is sunny and cheerful, with music, street life, and a friendly atmosphere that makes it easy to enjoy.
